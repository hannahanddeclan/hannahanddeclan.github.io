Wedding Ceremony Update (29th July)

Hi everyone!

We have a small update about the wedding ceremony after speaking to the church this week. They have requested that we continue to follow some restrictions, as follows:

  • Wear a mask while moving to and from your seats, like you would have done in a restaurant
  • Wear a mask while singing
  • Social distancing while within the church (more details below)

Part of their request was to continue to have social distancing measures between support bubbles while inside the church, so we have done our best to split the pews into groups. When you arrive, please take a look at the sign at the door which will tell you where to sit.

But the most important thing we can all do to keep everyone safe is to take a lateral flow test to make sure none of us have the virus before we come to the ceremony, and we really appreciate everybody taking the time to take one.

Thanks, and see you in a week!

Hannah and Declan

Wedding Update around Covid restrictions (19th July)

Following the official “reopening” and removal of all legal restrictions today, we are looking forward to going ahead with our wedding on 5th August!

We would like to have as normal a wedding as possible with dancing and no social distancing etc, but we still want the wedding to be safe and we’d really appreciate everyone’s help with this.

We’d really appreciate it if everyone could take a lateral flow test a day or two before the wedding to make sure we’re all covid-free. We know it’s not ideal, but with all of us taking a test we know we can be more relaxed celebrating together.

Taking these precautions should mean none of us are required to wear a mask around each other, but we are happy if anyone would still like to wear one. We’d also like to mention that we’re not the only guests at the hotel, so if people could please wear masks and use hand sanitiser when moving in and out of areas shared with other hotel guests that would be greatly appreciated.

Thank you so much for your understanding! If anyone wants to talk to us about this or ask any questions, please drop either of us a message.

We look forward to seeing you all in a couple of weeks! Lots of love, Hannah and Declan

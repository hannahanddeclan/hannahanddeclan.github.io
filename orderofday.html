Ceremony

The ceremony will be starting at 11.30am, and the vicar has asked all our guests to try to arrive by 11.20am so he can welcome you and run through a few things before the ceremony begins.

Photographs

After the ceremony we will be having formal photographs in the church grounds and confetti throwing before heading off to the hotel. If you'd prefer to head off and do your own thing until the drinks reception that's no problem, just let us know beforehand!

Drinks

Starting at 1.30pm at Armathwaite Hall we have arranged canapes and a few drinks for everyone. Hopefully there's enough selection for everyone to have a drink they enjoy, but if you'd prefer something else their cocktail bar will be open for you to go and purchase any other drinks.

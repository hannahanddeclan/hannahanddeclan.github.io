Self-Catering Options
All of the self-catering options we have found are for week-long stays starting on Friday 30th July or Saturday 31st July. There will be shorter stays available elsewhere, we just haven't researched those. Aside from Tallentire Hall, the options are all from the Sally's Cottages company. There are multiple other companies doing holiday cottage rental options, including 'Cumbrian', 'Lake Lovers', 'Sykes Cottages' and 'Airbnb'; however, we know Sally's is trustworthy and we're hoping to get a discount on their properties so we've only researched theirs. If you want to book any of the self-catering options let us know and we'll ask about a discount for you!
